"There is no new thing under the sun", so why create anything? There are countless lines of text put into creation by human hands. What could you possibly have to write that is important enough to add to our word-crowded world?<!-- end-preview --> This is one of the foremost blocking concepts I struggle with as I motivate myself to write.
